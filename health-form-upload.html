Upload Optional Health Forms

The Optional Health forms are available as fillable PDF documents using the links below.

Form F - Medication Authorization
If any medication dispensing (OTC or prescription) is necessary during camp activities and band trips.
For prescription medication, physician’s signature is required.

Form G - Inhaler Authorization
If it is necessary for a student to carry an inhaler. Physician’s signature is required.

Form H - EpiPen Authorization
If it is necessary for a student to carry an EpiPen. Physician’s signature is required.

After you have downloaded the fillable PDF form:

  • Complete the "Parent/Guardian to Complete" section
  • Have physician complete/sign the "Physician to Complete" section
  • Sign/date the Parent Signature line
  • Scan the document and save as a PDF with the filename format:
    LastFirst-FormX.pdf (for example, SousaJohn-FormC.pdf)
  • Click on the Upload Signed Forms button below. You will be taken to a Dropbox page where you will upload the files one at a time. You do not need to have a Dropbox account. Please upload each form as a separate PDF, not as one combined document.
